About Variscite

For over two decades Variscite has developed, produced and manufactured a powerful range of System on Module solutions, consistently setting market benchmarks in terms of speed and innovation. The company’s portfolio is based on leading SoC vendors including NXP, Qualcomm and Texas Instruments. All Variscite production is performed at fully ISO 13485, ISO 9001 and ISO 14001 compliant facilities, satisfying international customer and regulatory requirements for a broad range of industries including medical devices and related services. Variscite’s in-house production facilities are equipped with the most advanced SMT machines that ensure punctual deliveries and high-quality products.

For more information, visit https://variscite.com
